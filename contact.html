Contact

Feedback is welcome—especially if you spot a bug, confusing wording, or want a feature added.

How to reach us

For now, the simplest setup is to use a dedicated email address for the site:

  • Email: support@mortgageriskcalculator.com
  • Subject ideas: “Bug”, “Suggestion”, “Content correction”, “Ad/partnership”

What to include

  • What page you were on (Tool / Guide / Rates / etc.)
  • What you expected to happen
  • What actually happened
  • Optional: a screenshot (remove personal details)

Important note

We can’t provide personalized financial advice. If you’re making major decisions, consider speaking with a qualified professional who can review your full situation.
