If I Lose My Job, What Happens?

Most people don’t fail immediately. They fail when runway runs out. Stress testing shows the timeline.

Model Job Loss

The timeline most people underestimate

  • Month 1–2: Savings mask the problem
  • Month 3–5: Stress and forced decisions
  • Month 6+: Defaults and damage accelerate

What actually keeps you safe

  • Cash runway
  • Low fixed obligations
  • Early action

How to prepare before it happens

  1. Stress test income loss.
  2. Identify the burn rate.
  3. Build runway above panic threshold.
