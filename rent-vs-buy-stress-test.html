Rent vs Buy: A Stress Test Perspective

Buying builds equity. Renting preserves flexibility. Stress testing shows which one survives pressure better.

Test Buying Scenarios

The missing variable: downside risk

Most rent vs buy calculators assume everything goes according to plan. Stress testing asks: what happens when it doesn’t?

Why renting can be safer (sometimes)

  • Lower fixed commitments
  • Faster exit in income disruption
  • No repair or escrow shock

Why buying can still win

  • Strong margin and savings buffer
  • Stable income
  • Long time horizon

How to compare properly

  1. Stress test the mortgage side.
  2. Look at runway under job loss or expense spikes.
  3. Compare flexibility, not just cost.
